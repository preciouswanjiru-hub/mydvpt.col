Gallery

Welcome to the visual chronicle of Fork & Knife. This gallery offers an intimate glance behind our kitchen doors, celebrating the raw rhythm of dinner service, the architectural ambient design of our dining spaces, and the painstaking artisanal precision poured into our signature East African-European fusion plates. Every frame tells a story of heritage reimagined.
