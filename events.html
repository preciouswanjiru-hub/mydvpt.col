Capacity Guide

The Bistro: Up to 30 Guests

Main Hall: Up to 75 Guests

Full Venue buy-out: Up to 110 Guests

Guidelines

Booking

  1. Customers must complete booking form
  2. A deposit of 30% is required to secure spot as per venue desired
  3. Bookings are made on first come first served basis

Cancellation and Refund Policy

  1. Cancellation is allowed up to 3 days before event
  2. Deposits are non-refundable
  3. Refunds depend on time of cancellation

Security

  1. Security may be provided for events with guests exceeding 100
  2. We are not liable for lost or stolen items unless negligence is proven

We encourage our clients to treat all staff with respect and follow the above guidelines to ensure better service.
