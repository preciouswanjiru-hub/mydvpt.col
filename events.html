Guidelines

Booking

Customers must complete booking form. A deposit of 30% is required to secure spot as per venue desired. Bookings are made on first come first served basis.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

Cancellation is allowed up to 3 days before event. Deposits are non-refundable. Refunds depend on time of cancellation.

We encourage our clients to treat all staff with respect and follow the above guidelines to ensure better service.
