About Us

Here at Fork & Knife we believe the best food brings people together. We richly intergrate African and European cusines to create a unique dining experience and where do we get this inspiration? Our Kenyan roots of love for food and diverse culinary culture. This is what makes Fork & Knife the gem you see today. We are passionate about delivering an unforgettable dining experience.

Discover Fork & Knife

Fork & Knife is located in the heart of downtown, making it easily accessible for both locals and tourists.We are located in Westlands, Nairobi.

We are a passionate team of food enthusiasts dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience. Our mission is to create a welcoming atmosphere where guests can enjoy delicious meals made from the freshest ingredients.

Our founder, J.Mathaai, has over 20 years of experience in the culinary industry. With a background in fine dining and a love for innovative cuisine, he has created a unique menu that combines classic flavors with modern twists.

Our management team consists of experienced professionals who are committed to ensuring that every aspect of our restaurant runs smoothly. From our head chef to our front-of-house staff, we work together to provide an unforgettable dining experience for our guests.
